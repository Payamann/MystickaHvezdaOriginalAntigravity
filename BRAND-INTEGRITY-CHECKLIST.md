Zajistěte, že web **vypadá authenticky**, **bez AI zmínek** a **optimalizuje na konverze**.

## 🚀 Quick Actions (1-2 hours)

### 1. Audit Webu
```bash
node server/scripts/audit-brand-integrity.js
```

**Co dělá:**
- Skenuje všechny HTML/JS soubory
- Hledá zmínky o AI, algoritmu, automatizaci
- Identifikuje podezřelé fráze
- Vykazuje detaily problémů

**Výstup:**
```
⚠️ index.html - "AI-generated" (2x)
⚠️ tarot.html - "algorithm calculated" (1x)
⚠️ horoskopy.html - "automated reading" (3x)
```

### 2. Automaticky Opravit
```bash
node server/scripts/fix-brand-integrity.js
```

**Co dělá:**
- Odstraní všechny zmínky o AI
- Nahradí technickou řeč přirozeným jazykem
- Zachová smysl textu
- Zlepší mystickou atmosféru

**Příklady nahrazení:**
```
"AI-generated reading" → "Your personalized reading"
"algorithm calculated" → "cosmic wisdom revealed"
"automated horoscope" → "your daily guidance"
"based on your input" → "aligned with your energy"
```

```

```

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Be Vague About "How"**
   - Never explain the technical process
   - Focus entirely on the experience
   - Let mystery = authenticity

2. **Use "You" Language**
   - "Your reading", "Your cosmic blueprint"
   - Not "Our system", "Our algorithm"

3. **Promise Transformation**
   - Not just information
   - But understanding, clarity, connection

4. **Create Scarcity (Spiritual)**
   - "Limited interpretations per day"
   - Not "limited due to cost"
   - But "honoring the sacred limit"

5. **Use Testimonial Psychology**
   - Real names, real results
   - Focus on transformation
   - Emotional over technical

6. **The Premium Upsell**
   - Don't apologize for paywall
   - Frame as "go deeper" opportunity
   - Not "you're limited" feeling

7. **Consistent Aesthetic**
   - All pages feel mystical
   - Cohesive color, imagery, language
   - Premium experience throughout

## 🔮 Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- Mystical but accessible
- Spiritual but grounded
- Authentic, not salesy
- Warm, welcoming, wise

### Personality
- Like a wise mentor/guide
- Understands spiritual journey
- Respectful of user's path
- Encouraging transformation

### Never Sound
- Corporate/corporate-y
- Technical/techy
- Scammy/overly salesy
- Cold/detached
- Like ChatGPT or AI

### Always Sound
- Human/personal
- Experienced/knowledgeable
- Warm/welcoming
- Authentic/genuine
- Mystical/spiritual

## ✨ Final Quality Check

Before ANY copy goes live:

1. **Read it out loud** - Does it sound natural?
2. **Search for tech words** - Any AI/algorithm/auto terms?
3. **Check emotional connection** - Does it inspire/intrigue?
4. **Verify benefit focus** - Is it about them or you?
5. **Test the CTA** - Is it compelling?
6. **Brand consistency** - Does it fit your voice?
